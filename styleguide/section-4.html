4 #permalink Cards

Cards are any content where the following is present and intended as a summary/jumping off point to investigate content further.

Cards currently include:

  • Image
  • Title
  • Summary
  • CTA to learn more

4.1 #permalink Card

A card should (whenever possible, like any pattern) leverage preexisting styles.

Example

Article Title

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Nulla fugiat...

Read More 
<article class="card">
	<img src="http://placehold.it/300" alt="card image" />
	<h3>Article Title</h3>
	<p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Nulla fugiat...</p>
	<a href="#">Read More</a>
</article>
